Classic Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 3 cups mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. Brown ground beef in a skillet. Drain excess fat and add marinara sauce. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, salt, and pepper.
  5. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in a baking dish. Layer noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella, and meat sauce. Repeat layers, ending with sauce and mozzarella on top. Sprinkle Parmesan cheese.
  6. Bake for 30-35 minutes until cheese is bubbly and golden.
  7. Let cool for 10 minutes before serving.
